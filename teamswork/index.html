On September 2nd, Manchester United announced the partnership with HCL Technologies, as thier digital transformation partner.

TeamsWork is a marketing driven app, launched to promote the awareness of this partnership among the leaders of the top companies in the world.

The app allows people to share messages with their peers, recognizing them for their idiosyncrasies while comparing their skills with those of players on the soccer field of Manchester United.

I managed the development of the app, with two excellent design and development agencies, HCL works with.
